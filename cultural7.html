Conclusion

Love is patient, blind… Love is unconditional. Whether its two men like Cameron and Mitchell in Modern Family, two women, or a man and a woman, love is one of the strongest emotions, and without it, humans would be damned. Acceptance is something today’s world needs to work on. You can’t help whom you fall in love with. Sometimes girls fall in love with guys who are jerks, and other times, two men fall completely in love and spend the rest of their lives together, happily. Either way, love is love, and when one person loves another, nobody can ever take that feeling away. It shouldn’t be a crime to love somebody; everybody deserves the right to do so. /p>
