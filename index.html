All About Me:

Hello! My name is Jenna Nabozny, I am a freshman here at MSU studying Psychology. Although it's not the best major, I do have a plan for after. Once I graduate, I want to go to grad school and become a mental health therapist for people 18 and under. I am really looking forward to having a rewarding career like that where I help people on a daily basis.

My birthday is October 4th, and I was born in 1995, making me 18 years old. I have a brother and a sister, both who are in their mid twenties, making me the youngest of the family. I have the cutest little dog named Oliver. He is a Cavalier King Charles Spaniel, and I love him to death!
